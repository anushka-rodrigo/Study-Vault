# StudyVault – AI Powered Note Organizer

StudyVault is a mobile app that helps students bring their scattered study materials — PDFs, lecture slides, and photos of handwritten notes — into one organized, centralized place. On top of file management, it adds AI-powered summarization and quiz generation so students can review their material faster and retain more of it, especially during exam periods.

## Problem Statement

Students today rely mostly on digital learning materials rather than physical books, but these files usually end up scattered across WhatsApp, Google Drive, and local storage. Poorly named, duplicated, and disorganized files make it hard to find what's needed, which becomes especially stressful during exam periods when quick access to the right material matters most. On top of that, students spend a lot of time reading through PDFs with no easy way to summarize or prioritize key content. StudyVault addresses this by giving students a single place to organize their notes, pin important files, and generate AI summaries of their learning materials.

## Objectives

**Primary**
- Provide a centralized platform to store and organize study materials (PDFs and images)
- Allow students to manage their files efficiently
- Integrate AI-powered summarization for quick understanding

**Secondary**
- Allow students to mark important notes
- Enable secure user authentication
- Improve student productivity by reducing time spent searching for and reading through learning materials
- Help students study using AI-summarized notes
